What is Lorem Ipsum?

A deep dive into the history and importance of the most famous placeholder text.

The Origins

Lorem Ipsum dates back to the 1500s, when an unknown printer scrambled a type specimen book to create this standardized dummy text. It has survived not only five centuries, but also the leap into electronic typesetting.

"Lorem Ipsum comes from sections 1.10.32 and 1.10.33 of 'de Finibus Bonorum et Malorum' (The Extremes of Good and Evil) by Cicero, written in 45 BC."

Why Use Lorem Ipsum

Lorem Ipsum is often used because it provides a realistic pattern of letters, making it perfect for previewing layouts without being distracted by meaningful content.

  • Focus on design: It allows you to focus on the look and feel of a project without the distraction of readable content.
  • Neutral text: The text is nonsensical, so it doesn't trigger user bias or comprehension.
  • Customizable: You can generate as much or as little Lorem Ipsum as needed for any project.
